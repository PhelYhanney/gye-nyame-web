    Who We Are

    Gye Nyame MID 3 LTD Was Originally founded in 2015 as a small agro product broker under Kingdom Exim Ghana Ltd, it was officially incorporated by the Registrar General in 2023 after splitting off in 2020.

    With over nine years of extensive experience in the agribusiness industry, we are highly results-driven and goal-oriented agribusiness dealers who continuously strive to achieve excellence in every aspect of our operations.

    We pride ourselves on our unwavering commitment to meeting and exceeding high standards, ensuring that our clients receive top-quality products and services.

    Our team is adept at working efficiently under pressure, maintaining a calm and focused approach even in the face of challenging situations. We excel in environments where deadlines are crucial, delivering exceptional outcomes within tight time frames while never compromising on quality or attention to detail.
